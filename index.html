About Me

Hi, Im Dominykas (Dominic) and I am a front-end developer and software engineering graduate based in London, UK. I originally began my coding journey working with Java and Python to build small desktop applications at university but over time I noticed that I would always enjoy developing the front-end of an application more. I often volunteered myself for the role in any programming-related group work at university. After graduating, I decided to pursue front-end development as a serious full-time career.

To date, I have experience using various modern-day front-end tools and technologies such as the ones listed below but I am always trying to learn more relevant tech on a need-to-know basis. Although I specialise in front-end development, I recognise that this is an ever-growing space and therefore I make an effort to keep up and advance my knowledge by diving into other areas such as: UI/UX practices and back-end (something that I am still currently working on).

Outside of the realm of development, I am also familiar with agile (scrum). I have experience participating in and leading various types of meetings and I am not afraid to take on new responsibilities outside of my day-to-day ones, if required. Overall, I am a driven team-player who is dedicated to progress and success. I always strive to meet set requirements and go above and beyond to ensure that the most optimal results are achieved.
